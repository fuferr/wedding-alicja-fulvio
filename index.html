Napoli Tips
✦

Travel · Stay · Eat · Explore

✈️

Getting There

By Air — Recommended

Napoli Airport (NAP)

Direct flights from most European cities. ~20 min by taxi to the centre. Alibus shuttle bus also available (€5).

By Air — Alternative

Salerno Costa d'Amalfi (QSR)

A smaller airport south of Naples. Beautiful location but 1+ hour from the city — factor in travel time when planning.

By Train

From Rome

Frecciarossa from Roma Termini — just 1h 10min, from €20. Scenic and stress-free if flying into Rome.

🚕

Getting Around Naples

Recommended

itTaxi App

The official Naples taxi app. Easy to book, reliable and uses licensed taxis with fixed or metered rates. Download before you travel.

Phone booking

Taxi Napoli — 8888

The classic Naples taxi number. Call 081 8888 to book a licensed taxi by phone — great when you're without signal or data.

Note on Uber

Uber is limited here

Uber operates in Naples but is significantly more expensive and has very limited availability. We recommend itTaxi or Taxi Napoli 8888 instead.

🏨

Where to Stay

Luxury

Grand Hotel Vesuvio

Iconic five-star on the seafront. Views of Castel dell'Ovo and Vesuvius. An unforgettable experience.

Boutique — our favourite

Costantinopoli 104

A Liberty-style palazzo in the historic centre with a pool and garden. Hidden gem, beautifully intimate.

Mid-range

Hotel Piazza Bellini

Charming hotel on one of Naples' most beautiful piazzas. Great breakfast and right in the artistic quarter.

🍕

Where to Eat

Pizza — iconic

L'Antica Pizzeria da Michele

The most famous pizza in the world. Margherita or Marinara only. Arrive early — queues are long and worth it.

Fine dining

Il Comandante

Michelin-starred atop the Romeo Hotel. Stunning bay views. A truly special occasion dinner — book ahead.

Aperitivo

Caffè Gambrinus

Naples' most historic café, open since 1860. Perfect for a Campari spritz or espresso at the bar.

🏛

Things to Do

Must-do

Pompeii & Vesuvius

30 min by train (Circumvesuviana). Walk the ancient streets and hike Vesuvius for views of a lifetime.

Day trip

Amalfi Coast

Ferry or car along the world's most dramatic coastline. Positano, Ravello, Amalfi — unmissable.

Island escape

Capri or Ischia

Ferry from Molo Beverello (~1h). Capri is glamorous; Ischia is lush and thermal. Both are paradise.

A Few Tips from Us

☕ Coffee

Espresso standing at the bar costs €1–1.20. Cappuccino after noon is frowned upon by locals — but you're on holiday!

🌡 Weather in September

Warm and sunny — expect 25–30°C. Evenings can be breezy near the water. Pack a light layer for the nights.

🛵 Getting around safely

Naples is very safe for tourists in the main areas. Keep bags close in busy streets and enjoy the city with confidence.

The Ceremony

Chiesa Santa Maria
del Faro a Marechiaro

A jewel of Posillipo perched above the sea — this intimate and luminous church has watched over Naples for centuries. With the Mediterranean as its backdrop, our vows will be spoken in one of the most romantic settings imaginable.

⏰  Ceremony begins at 15:30
🏛
The Reception

Villa Doria D'Angri

A majestic neoclassical villa in the Vomero hills, with sweeping panoramic views of the Bay of Naples and Vesuvius. Its grand terraces and ornate gardens set the stage for an unforgettable evening under the stars.

⏰  Reception from 17:00
Dress Code
✦
Day 1 — Friday 11 September

Formal Elegance

👔
Men — Suit required. Tie is optional but welcomed. Dark or light suits both work beautifully in the Neapolitan summer.
👗
Women — Long dress. Flowing gowns, elegant maxi dresses or formal floor-length styles. Soft and Mediterranean palettes encouraged.
🎨
Think creams, dusty blues, sage greens, terracotta and warm golds. Please avoid all-white and all-black.

Stiletto heels may struggle on historic cobblestone — block heels or wedges are a practical and stylish choice for the church.

Day 2 — Saturday 12 September

Linen & Beach Cocktail

👕
Men — Linen trousers and a relaxed linen or cotton shirt. Casual chic — think Capri boat trip meets coastal terrace lunch.
👙
Women — Cocktail dress, midi dress, linen co-ord or beach-elegant separates. Sun hat welcome at the lido!
🌊
We'll be spending time at the sea — comfortable, breathable and beautiful. Bring a swimsuit if you'd like to take a dip!

Day 2 is relaxed and joyful. Dress for the Mediterranean summer — easy, breezy and lovely.

Day 1 Friday, 11 September

15:00

Guests Arrive at the Church

Please be seated at Chiesa Santa Maria del Faro a Marechiaro by 15:15. The church doors open from 15:00.

15:30

The Ceremony

A religious ceremony in the luminous church of Marechiaro, overlooking the sea. Conducted in Italian and English.

17:00

Arrival at Villa Doria D'Angri

Guests are welcomed at the magnificent Villa Doria D'Angri with aperitivo, Prosecco and canapés on the panoramic terrace.

18:30

Cocktail Hour

Drinks, light bites and the golden Neapolitan sunset over the Bay of Naples and Mount Vesuvius. An hour you will not forget.

20:00

Wedding Dinner

A grand Neapolitan dinner — long tables, seasonal cuisine, fine wines and heartfelt toasts under the stars of the villa gardens.

22:00

First Dance & Celebrations

First dance, cake cutting, and an evening of music, laughter and dancing.

00:30

End of Evening

Transfer arrangements available back to city-centre hotels. Arrivederci — until tomorrow!

Good to Know

Ceremony language

The ceremony will be conducted in Italian and English.

Unplugged ceremony

We kindly ask for phones away during the vows. Our photographer will capture every moment.

Children

Children are warmly welcome. Please note the number of little guests when you RSVP.

Dietary needs

All dietary requirements are collected in the RSVP — we will accommodate everyone.

Getting between venues

The church and villa are both in Naples. We recommend arranging a taxi — journey is approx. 15–20 min.

Day 2 Saturday, 12 September

14:00

Lido Time

Join us for an afternoon by the sea at Baia delle Rocce Verdi — a beautiful lido on the Posillipo coast. Swimming, sun, good company and cold drinks.

18:00

Pizza After-Party — Nonno's Garden

An informal and joyful gathering at the family's garden — pizza, laughter and the last golden light of the weekend. The perfect way to end the celebrations together.

23:00

Arrivederci

End of celebrations. Thank you for being part of our story — we love you all.

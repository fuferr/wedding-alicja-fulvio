Dress Code
✦

Elegant Mediterranean

We invite you to dress in the spirit of a Southern Italian summer celebration. Think flowing silhouettes, linen fabrics, soft colours — creams, whites, dusty blues, terracottas and warm neutrals. Formal but coastal. Beautiful but comfortable.

Please avoid all-black and bright neons. Stiletto heels may struggle on cobblestone.

Day 1 Friday, 11 September

16:00

Guest Arrival

Guests are welcomed with an aperitivo and a glass of Prosecco in the gardens.

17:00

The Ceremony

An intimate and romantic civil ceremony overlooking the Bay of Naples.

18:00

Cocktail Hour

Drinks, canapés and the first golden hour of the evening on the terrace.

20:00

Wedding Dinner

A long Italian table, Neapolitan cuisine, fine wines and toasts under the stars.

22:30

Dancing & Celebration

First dance, cake cutting, and a night of music, laughter and joy.

01:00

End of Evening

Transfer arrangements available back to city-centre hotels.

Good to Know

Ceremony language

The ceremony will be conducted in Italian and English.

Children

Children are warmly welcome. Please indicate the number of little guests when you RSVP.

Photography

We kindly ask for an unplugged ceremony — please keep phones away during the vows.

Dietary needs

All dietary requirements are noted in the RSVP form — we will accommodate everyone.

Day 2 Saturday, 12 September

11:00

Morning Brunch

Time to explore Napoli before heading home. We'll share local tips on the website!
